Devlog: Tempo

Friday, Oct 26, 2018

As a supervillain, I would like to ensure that my minions and peons are working at maximum efficiency. The following post (and probably series) describes my journey in creating a tool that will help them stay productive. This leads me to a question: What tomatoes, egg timers, and university have in common? They are all part of Francesco Cirillo’s Pomodoro technique that aims to manage time and tasks efficiently. The basics are that you have indivisible units of working time separated by short breaks and that you focus solely on the task at hand during a working unit.
