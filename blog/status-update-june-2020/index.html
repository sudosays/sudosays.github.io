Status Update, June 2020

Sunday, Jun 21, 2020

This post is inspired by the status updates of Drew DeVault (who remains a large inspiration for keeping this blog as a whole). This entry is a bit late, but better than never. In future I think I will also try to update at the middle of each month. This is also a chance to add updates from my life that do not warrant a full post here or may seem unrelated to the spirit of this blog.

My last post to this blog was “Be Static To Go Faster” which dates back to the 25th of February this year. In the intervening months, many, many things have happened. I have been working towards the completion of my Master’s degree, drafted a whole bunch of posts only to abandon them later. These last few months of quarantine and lockdown have been incredibly turbulent and the time slipped by in the blink of an eye.

I do not have much else to report as of now. There are a few things in the works especially with respect to Tempo and other projects. However, I will be focusing mainly on my degree for the next while.

Until then, study hard and, stay evil.
