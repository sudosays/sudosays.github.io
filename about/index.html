About

The Fellowship of Evil Supervillains is a fictional organisation created and run by myself for fun and evil.

What you see here is the work of the propaganda arm of the Fellowship. The site itself is generated from Org files using Hugo and hosted via Github. We aim to make the content minimal, plain, and legible while making sure it is also really fast.

The content itself is published sporadically, but we also offer RSS feeds for the entire site, or a subset such as the blog, tags, and categories.

It is important to note that the pieces found here are living documents and as such are subject to refinement and expansion over time.
