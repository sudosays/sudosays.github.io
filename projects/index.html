Tempo

Thursday, Mar 5, 2020

This is my personal productivity app. It started from trying to merge the Pomodoro technique with a to-do list so that I could budget and plan my time. Other productivity apps I had used in the past featured either a timer only, or a to-do list, but not both. Furthermore, I use this to help keep me in a flow state by reducing the amount of effort I need to structure my work.
